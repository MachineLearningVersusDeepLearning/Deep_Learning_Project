
Self Driving Car - v3 fixed-small
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on March 10, 2020 at 12:15 PM GMT

It includes 15000 images.
Obstacles are annotated with bounding boxes in COCO format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 512x512 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
